Background

Percussive instruments require an impact. Sound is produced through the percussive action of one object stricking another. What are thought to be the oldest musical instruments in history come from this catagory. They can also be pitched or unpitched.

2 Main Types

  1. Membranophone
    • Have a thin layer that is stretched very tightly over a frame
    • Ex: Snare drum, tom drums
  2. Idiophone
    • Produce sound from the vibrations of the entire instrument when struck
    • Ex: Cymbals
